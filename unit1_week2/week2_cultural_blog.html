Pick one value and write a couple of sentences about why one of the values you chose is important to you.

Free time is the most important value to me. You may be thinking, "Wow, Kevin must be such a slacker", but I don't mean it in that way. While some people may feel accomplished after finishing a packed schedule at the end of the day, I'm content with just having a bit of free time in my day to unwind and do what I want to do. I consider myself an introvert, and introverts value their "alone time" highly. It's highly beneficial for my mental sanity and it gives me time to reflect on what I did during the day.
