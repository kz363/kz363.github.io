Cultural Blog #6

April 27, 2014 by Kevin

My most important value that I wrote about in the first cultural blog was free time. If I were feeling down at DBC, the best thing I can do is to either get some alone time or hang out with close friends. Personally, I'm not one to be negatively affected by stereotypes. When I last wrote about stereotype threat, I found that the best way to deal with it is to understand the stereotype, and I think I understand mine well. I'm also someone that just does my work without being distracted by what others think of me, so I don't think stereotype threat will affect me.
